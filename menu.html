Current Event

To celebrate the new opening of Catayo, we are happy to introduce the Tiger Pattern Boba Milk Tea to you! The tiger pattern is made of brown sugar and has a smooth taste. The boba of this tea is made by boiling tapioca starch and soaking honey. We cooperated with Taiwan's famous Boba tea shop Tiger Sugar and improved the recipe to be more delicious. During the opening period, the Tiger Pattern Milk Tea with original price of 6 dollars will be sold for 4 dollar per cup. The special offer is only until June 21, 2021. Please take your chance and taste the sweetness from Taiwan!

Health Reminder: Too much sugar is harmful to your health. Boba is not suitable for children under 8 years old.
When eating boba, please chew and swallow carefully.
